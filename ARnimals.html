Overview

ARCore is Google's platform for creating augmented reality experiences, allowing your phone to perceive its surroundings, comprehend the world, and engage with information. Certain APIs are compatible with both Android and iOS, facilitating shared AR experiences. The ARnimals app leverages ARCore to offer an interactive augmented reality experience.

Compatible Devices

ARCore is designed to work on a wide variety of qualified Android phones running Android 7.0 (Nougat) and later. A full list of all supported devices is available here.
